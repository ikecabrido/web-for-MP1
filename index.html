About the Philippines

The Philippines is a tropical paradise with more than 7,000 islands, where you can experience stunning beaches, diverse wildlife, vibrant culture, and friendly people. Whether you want to relax on the white sands of Boracay, dive with whale sharks in Donsol, explore the rice terraces of Banaue, or witness the chocolate hills of Bohol, the Philippines has something for everyone. The country also has a rich history and heritage, influenced by Spanish, American, and Asian cultures. You can visit the historic churches and forts of Manila, the colorful festivals and street food of Cebu, or the indigenous tribes and art of Mindanao. The Philippines is a destination that will make you fall in love with its beauty and charm.
