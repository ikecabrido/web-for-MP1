About Mindanao

Mindanao is the second-largest island in the Philippines, where you can experience the rich cultural diversity, stunning natural wonders, and adventure activities of the southern part of the country. Mindanao is home to various ethnic groups, such as the Maranao, Tausug, Maguindanao, and Lumad, who have their own distinct traditions, languages, and arts. Mindanao is also a paradise for nature lovers and adventurers, who can enjoy the tropical beaches of Samal Island, the surfing waves of Siargao Island, the volcanic springs of Camiguin Island, and the majestic waterfalls of Iligan City. Mindanao is also where you can find the highest peak in the country, Mount Apo, which offers a challenging trek and a breathtaking view.

Places to visit in Mindanao

Aliwagwag Falls Eco Park

Aliwagwag Falls is a majestic sight of 84 tiers of waterfalls that reach 1,110 feet in height. It is the tallest waterfall in the Philippines and a UNESCO heritage site. Surrounded by rich biodiversity, it is a source of life and a wonderful destination for nature lovers .

Location: Compostela - Cateel Rd, Cateel, Davao Oriental

Enchanted River

Enchanted River is a mysterious spring river that emerges from the jungle and flows into the sea. Its deep blue waters are crystal clear and inviting. A popular attraction in Mindanao, it offers swimming, snorkeling, and diving adventures. A hidden gem of nature's beauty.

Location: Barangay Talisay, Hinatuan, Surigao del Sur

Learn more
Siargao Island

Siargao Island is a teardrop-shaped island in the Philippine Sea. It is the surfing capital of the Philippines with world-class waves and stunning beaches. It also offers other attractions such as mangroves, lagoons, caves, and waterfalls. A paradise for adventure seekers and nature lovers

Location: Surigao del Norte, Philippines.

Learn more
Pink Beach

Pink Beach is a rare and beautiful beach in Great Santa Cruz Island, Zamboanga City. It has a pink hue from the crushed red corals mixed with the white sand. It is a perfect place for swimming, snorkeling, and relaxing. A must-see attraction in the Philippines

Location: Basilan Strait, Zamboanga City

Learn more
Dahilayan Adventure Park

Dahilayan Adventure Park is a mountain resort in Bukidnon that offers thrilling rides and activities. It has Asia's longest dual zipline, a dropzone, a pendulum swing, and more. It is a fun and exciting place for adventure seekers and nature lovers

Location: Brgy. Dahilayan, Manolo Fortich Bukidnon, Philippines

Learn more
Philippine Eagle Centre

Philippine Eagle Center is a conservation breeding facility for the endangered Philippine Eagle and other wildlife. It is located at the foothills of Mt. Apo in Davao City. It showcases the country's forest ecosystem and biodiversity. It is a popular destination for nature lovers and wildlife enthusiasts

Location: Calinan-Baguio-Cadalian Road, Davao City

Learn more
Eden Nature Park

Eden Nature Park is a mountain resort in Davao City that offers various attractions and activities for nature lovers. It has pine forests, gardens, trails, pools, ziplines, and cultural shows. It also has cozy accommodations and delicious food. It is a relaxing and refreshing getaway

Location: Southern end of Davao City

Learn more
Maria Cristina Falls

Maria Cristina Falls is a majestic waterfall of the Agus River in Iligan City, Mindanao. It is also known as the twin falls because of a rock that splits the flow. It is a source of hydroelectric power and a popular tourist attraction

Location: Iligan City

Learn more
Dinagat Islands

Dinagat Islands is a young island-province at the northern tip of Mindanao. It has a great bio-diversity, abundant natural wonders and rich resources. It has white sandy beaches, colossal rock formations, caves, waterfalls and lakes. It is a hidden gem for nature lovers and adventurers

Location: Northeast of Mindanao
